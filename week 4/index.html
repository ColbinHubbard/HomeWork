3 of the greatest point guards....and more

First point guard, Wardell "Stephen" Curry

Stephen Curry is first on this list, not because of his championship accolades, but for being the Greatest shooter of ALL-Time. His all time 3 pt percentage is 43.1 percent, he also holds the record for the most 3 pointers made. He is a two time regular MVP award winner, let alone the fist unanimous MVP award winner. Many people feels he's finally received his flowers by finally winning NBA finals MVP award. Here is a video of some of his greatest highlights. link here

Second point guard,(and one of my all time favorites) Kyrie Irving

Kyrie Irving, one of the most skilled point guards ever. Let alone the greatest ball handler to touch the basketball, is one of the most creative ball handlers recorded in NBA history. Kyrie won rookie of the year in 2011-2012 season, with the stat line of 18.5 ppg, 5.4 assist, and 3.7 rebounds per game. These are great stats for a 6'2 ft rookie point guard. His ALL-Time stats are 23.1 ppg on 47 shooting percentage.

Third, we have Chris "CP3" Paul

Chris Paul is one of the greatest pass first point guards, a very traditional role for his position. He is also a rookie of the year award winner, he is third in the all time assist leaders.He is a five time league assist leader and a six time league steals leader. Link to CP3 giving 15 assist
